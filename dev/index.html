Paper production
Paper for Recycling is a key raw material for the paper industry. Through a series of operations this raw material is converted into new paper and board products.

Side stream
A significant amount of the raw material is lost from the process in the form of side streams. Disposing of these side streams often means additional costs for the paper mill.

Valuable components within these side streams can, however, find applications and, possibly, be turned into profits.
