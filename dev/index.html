During a series of operations the raw material is transformed into a new paper end product.
A significant amount of the raw material leaves the process as a side stream.
Disposing these side streams often brings costs for the paper mill.
However, the valuable components in these side streams can be turned into profits.

There are many opportunities for the paper industry to transform side streams into valuable products. How? By implementing innovative technologies.
